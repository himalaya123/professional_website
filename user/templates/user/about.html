My Story..

Hi ! My name is Afazal Khan. I am one of the leading SEO expert in India with 10+ years of experience. I am a computer science engineer who is passionate about search engine optimisation.

From my college days I was passionate about search engine optimisation but never thought of working in this domain. During this time i got attracted towards SEO. Learned SEO on my own and started blogging.

Soon after my graduation ,I was offered a salary of Rs 5000 per month. I thought it as a good opportunity to follow my passion. I accepted the job offer. And from there my SEO journey started. I spend some years working as a digital marketing consultant in leading startups of Mumbai and after started my own venture.

There is no looking back after that.Now I am one of the leading digital marketing consultant in Bhoapl who heads a team of 5 people.
